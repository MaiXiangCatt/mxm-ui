Button 按钮 ​

常用的操作按钮

基础用法 ​

使用type, plain, round和circle来定义按钮样式。

<template>
  <div>
    <p>
      <mxm-button>Default</mxm-button>
      <mxm-button type="primary">Primary</mxm-button>
      <mxm-button type="success">Success</mxm-button>
      <mxm-button type="info">Info</mxm-button>
      <mxm-button type="warning">Warning</mxm-button>
      <mxm-button type="danger">Danger</mxm-button>
    </p>

    <p>
      <mxm-button plain>Plain</mxm-button>
      <mxm-button type="primary" plain>Primary</mxm-button>
      <mxm-button type="success" plain>Success</mxm-button>
      <mxm-button type="info" plain>Info</mxm-button>
      <mxm-button type="warning" plain>Warning</mxm-button>
      <mxm-button type="danger" plain>Danger</mxm-button>
    </p>

    <p>
      <mxm-button round>Round</mxm-button>
      <mxm-button type="primary" round>Primary</mxm-button>
      <mxm-button type="success" round>Success</mxm-button>
      <mxm-button type="info" round>Info</mxm-button>
      <mxm-button type="warning" round>Warning</mxm-button>
      <mxm-button type="danger" round>Danger</mxm-button>
    </p>

    <p>
      <mxm-button icon="search" circle />
      <mxm-button type="primary" icon="edit" circle />
      <mxm-button type="success" icon="check" circle />
      <mxm-button type="info" icon="message" circle />
      <mxm-button type="warning" icon="star" circle />
      <mxm-button type="danger" icon="trash" circle />
    </p>
  </div>
</template>

禁用状态 ​

可以使用disabled属性来定义按钮是否被禁用，该属性接收一个boolean类型的值。

<template>
  <div>
    <p>
      <mxm-button>Default</mxm-button>
      <mxm-button type="primary">Primary</mxm-button>
      <mxm-button type="success">Success</mxm-button>
      <mxm-button type="info">Info</mxm-button>
      <mxm-button type="warning">Warning</mxm-button>
      <mxm-button type="danger">Danger</mxm-button>
    </p>
    <p>
      <mxm-button disabled>Default</mxm-button>
      <mxm-button type="primary" disabled>Primary</mxm-button>
      <mxm-button type="success" disabled>Success</mxm-button>
      <mxm-button type="info" disabled>Info</mxm-button>
      <mxm-button type="warning" disabled>Warning</mxm-button>
      <mxm-button type="danger" disabled>Danger</mxm-button>
    </p>
  </div>
</template>

图标按钮 ​

可以使用icon属性来为按钮添加图标, 可以在Icon 组件中找到所需图标。

<template>
  <div>
    <mxm-button type="primary" icon="edit"></mxm-button>
    <mxm-button type="primary" icon="share"></mxm-button>
    <mxm-button type="primary" icon="trash"></mxm-button>
    <mxm-button type="primary" icon="search">Search</mxm-button>
    <mxm-button type="primary">
      Upload<mxm-icon icon="upload" style="margin-left: 6px;"></mxm-icon>
    </mxm-button>
  </div>
</template>

按钮组 ​

以按钮组的方式出现，常用于多项类似操作。使用<mxm-button-group>对多个按钮分组。

<template>
  <p>
    <mxm-button-group>
      <mxm-button type="primary" icon="arrow-left">Previous Page</mxm-button>
      <mxm-button type="primary">
        Next Page<mxm-icon icon="arrow-right" style="margin-left: 6px" />
      </mxm-button>
    </mxm-button-group>
  </p>
  <p>
    <mxm-button-group type="warning" size="small">
      <mxm-button type="primary" icon="edit" />
      <mxm-button type="primary" icon="share"/>
      <mxm-button type="primary" icon="trash" />
    </mxm-button-group>
  </p>
</template>

加载状态按钮 ​

通过设置loading属性来显示加载中状态。

TIP

可以使用 loading 插槽或 loadingIcon属性自定义您的loading图标。loading插槽的优先级高于loadingIcon属性。

<template>
  <div>
    <mxm-button type="primary" loading>Loading</mxm-button>
    <mxm-button type="primary" loading loading-icon="circle-notch">Loading</mxm-button>
    <mxm-button type="primary" loading>
      <template #loading>
        <mxm-icon icon="moon" spin style="margin-right: 6px;"></mxm-icon>
      </template>
      Loading
    </mxm-button>
  </div>
</template>

调整尺寸 ​

除默认大小，按钮组件还可以调整尺寸。使用size属性配置尺寸，可使用large和small两种值

<template>
  <div>
    <p>
      <mxm-button size="large">Large</mxm-button>
      <mxm-button>Default</mxm-button>
      <mxm-button size="small">Small</mxm-button>
      <mxm-button size="large" icon="search">Search</mxm-button>
      <mxm-button icon="search">Search</mxm-button>
      <mxm-button size="small" icon="search">Search</mxm-button>
    </p>
    <p>
      <mxm-button size="large" round>Large</mxm-button>
      <mxm-button round>Default</mxm-button>
      <mxm-button size="small" round>Small</mxm-button>
      <mxm-button size="large" icon="search" round>Search</mxm-button>
      <mxm-button icon="search" round>Search</mxm-button>
      <mxm-button size="small" icon="search" round>Search</mxm-button>
    </p>
    <p>
      <mxm-button size="large" icon="search" circle />
      <mxm-button icon="search" circle />
      <mxm-button size="small" icon="search" circle />
    </p>
  </div>
</template>

Tag ​

您可以自定义元素标签。例如，按钮，div，路由链接，nuxt链接。

div
a 

<template>
  <div>
    <mxm-button>button</mxm-button>
    <mxm-button tag="div" role="button" tabindex="0">div</mxm-button>
    <mxm-button
      type="primary"
      tag="a"
      href="https://github.com/MaiXiangCatt/mxm-ui"
      target="_blank"
      rel="noopener noreferrer"
    >
      a
    </mxm-button>
  </div>
</template>

节流模式 ​

可以使用useThrottle属性来启用节流模式，默认为true。

<template>
  <div>
    <mxm-button @click="btnClickHandler"> with throttle</mxm-button>
    <mxm-button @click="btnClickHandler" :use-throttle="false">without throttle</mxm-button>
  </div>
</template>

<script setup>
import { MxmMessage } from 'mxm-ui';
function btnClickHandler() {
  MxmMessage.info('button has been clicked')
}
</script>
